Welcome to King's

Welcome to King's College London. Whether you're a new undergraduate student starting in September, a postgraduate student continuing your study at King's, or a PhD candidate starting your course later in the year, these pages will give you the information you need to get started at King's.

Wherever you are in the world, our commitment to providing the very highest quality education is as strong as ever. Our tutors are looking forward to meeting you and welcoming you to our global community of learners.

A Virtual Welcome - September 2020

Welcome to King's takes place each year for all new students beginning their studies in September. This year Welcome to King's will take on a new virtual presence which offers a range of events and induction activities for our students across the globe. We want to ensure our students have the opportunity to participate in as many events as possible which is why Welcome to King's 2020 will take place over a two week period from the 14th to the 25th of September.

We encourage new students to regularly check the coronavirus pages on our website for the latest updates and advice.
